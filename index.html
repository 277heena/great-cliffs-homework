Introducing California's Greatest Cliffs

California’s greatest cliffs are four beautiful destinations located in Southern California: West Cliff, North Cliff, East Cliff, and South Cliff. Each one offers its own natural beauty, scenic lookouts, recreation areas, and unforgettable experiences.

Whether you enjoy nature walks, photography, camping, or peaceful ocean views, the Great Cliffs offer something for everyone. These locations are also easy to reach from Irvine, California, making them perfect for day trips and weekend adventures.

West Cliff is especially popular because of its close distance and relaxing coastal atmosphere. Explore all four cliffs through this site and discover activities, directions, and contact details.
